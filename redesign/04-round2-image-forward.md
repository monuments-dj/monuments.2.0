# Round 2: Image-Forward Directions (D / E / F)

DJ's feedback on round 1 (2026-07-01): too design/type-forward given how much strong
photography exists, and the brand book should NOT be an anchor. He wants new things,
new typography, imagery leading. Round 2 obeys that: no Archivo, no Space Mono, no
ink/denim/rose, photos carry every page.

The photo library (public/gallery: 100 selects; galaxy: 29; case-studies) is genuinely
cinematic: the chopper editorial series (p-068..p-077), red-dress hillside at sunset
(p-098), muscle-car dusk (p-061), b&w cowboy silhouette (p-013), white-ruffle on blue
(p-067), blinds-light portrait (p-049), crew photos (team.jpg, p-053, p-040).

## D · Feature — cinematic serif
Type: **Zodiak** (Fontshare display serif, italics as the accent) + **General Sans**.
Palette: bone #ECE7DD, espresso #171310, terracotta #B4552D.
Full-bleed photography, film-credit stats, split scenes with parallax, serif italic
pull-quote interlude on bone, drifting filmstrip. Exo Ape pacing wearing a serif voice.

## E · Poster — kinetic display
Type: **Clash Display** + **Satoshi** (Fontshare). Palette: black #0B0B0B, off-white,
international orange #FF4D00.
Cursor-drift photo collage behind the giant hero type, image marquee, poster-tile work
grid (hover flips the title band orange), solid-orange stats band. basement/Unseen lane.

## F · Contact Sheet — photo archive
Type: **Fragment Mono** + **Instrument Sans** (Google). Palette: photo-paper #F7F6F2,
ink, grease-pencil red #E0311A.
The ownable concept: DJ's decade as a photographer's archive. A 12-frame contact sheet
drawn from a 28-image pool that RESHUFFLES every visit (Adam Ho pattern), one frame
circled in red + a handwritten-style note, archive-ID work index (MC-2024-01), stats as
pinned editor's notes, career as annotated film strips ("Era 01, the systems years"),
crew as a polaroid. Micro-copy: "Refresh to reshuffle the sheet."

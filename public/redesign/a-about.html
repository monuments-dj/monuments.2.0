About · DJ Ramirez

A decade in the hard rooms.

I've spent ten years in rooms where the brief was wrong, the timeline was tight, and the stakes were real. I learned to love those rooms.

✳ The short version

I'm DJ Ramirez, a creative director. I build monuments with my friends.

I started in UX, learned the craft in agencies, and earned the title the loud way: on camera, on set, on the hook for the number. Now I run Monuments, a production studio and consultancy based between Los Angeles and Boise.

I plug in at the senior level: strategy, creative direction, integrated content. For brands that want to show up in culture and mean it, and for the agencies that serve them.

The road here

Four chapters
Chapter 01
UX Design

Where I learned systems. Research, wireframes, and why people do what they do. Every campaign I run still starts with that question.

Chapter 02
Agency Creative

Where I learned speed. Concepting daily, selling work in the room, shipping campaigns for global brands on real deadlines.

Chapter 03
Creative Director

Where I learned scale. On camera and behind it: leading teams of up to 50 contractors, growing agency revenue 4x, and directing an Emmy-winning film series.

Chapter 04
Monuments

Where it comes together. My own studio and consultancy. Me, Tess keeping the operation moving, and a trusted crew of makers who come in when the work calls for it.

The short version

I'm DJ Ramirez, a creative director. I build monuments with my friends.

I started in UX, learned the craft in agencies, and earned the title the loud way: on camera, on set, on the hook for the number. Now I run Monuments, a production studio and consultancy based between Los Angeles and Boise.

I plug in at the senior level: strategy, creative direction, integrated content. For brands that want to show up in culture and mean it, and for the agencies that serve them.

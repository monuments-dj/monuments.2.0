The short version

I'm DJ Ramirez, a creative director. I build monuments with my friends.

Ten years in the hard rooms: where the brief was wrong, the timeline was tight, and the stakes were real. I started in UX, learned speed in agencies, earned the title on camera and on the hook for the number.

Now I run Monuments, a production studio and consultancy between Los Angeles and Boise. I plug in at the senior level: strategy, creative direction, integrated content.
